About Me

Hello, I'm Megan! I'm a Michigan native and have spent most of my life living in either the Lansing or the metro Detroit areas. I'm a two-time graduate of Michigan State University and a diehard Spartan fan (Go Green!). I received my Bachelor of Arts in Elementary Education in 2009 and my Master of Arts in Educational Technology in 2011.

I spent three years teaching Kindergarten and first grade in East Lansing Public Schools before I was ultimately compelled by my Master's coursework and my passion for working with technology to pursue a career in the software industry. For the past seven years I have worked at various software companies in a customer success manager role where I have managed customer realtionships, onboarded new clients, developed and implemented training curriculum for clients, provided teachnical support to clients, as well as collected and curated client feedback to deliver to product teams.

It was while working closely with technical product managers and development teams as a customer success manager that my curiosity and interest in how software is actually developed began to grow. I started utilizing tools like Codeacademy® and freeCodeCamp to teach myself HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I also attended coding workshops put on by Grand Circus and taking online courses through SheCodes. Finally, in the fall of 2020, I decided to take the leap and pursue a new career in web development. I hope you enjoy my portfolio and please feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to work together. I'd love to hear from you!
